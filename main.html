Dungeon Map Starter
~by skathii

A starter snippet for a browser based dungeon game. Control the character using Up/Down/Left/Right keys.

The character spawns on the ladder tile and can only walk on the green tiles.

The minimap on the right of the page shows parts of the map that the character has visited. As you move around, more of the map will be clerar.
